Vegan Pasta

Description

The hearty and healthy vegan pasta dish. Using our very own gravy recipe we can bring together two brilliant recipes to create a culinary masterclass on a plate. This recipe is great for those who want something relatively quick but delicious and filling. This recipe serves 1.

Ingredients

  • Gravy
  • 300g Penne Pasta
  • Himalayan Salt
  • French Stir Fry
  • Mediterranean Olive Oil
  • 1x Potato

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200ÂºC
  2. Cut the potato into medium sized pieces.
  3. Drop the potato pieces into a bowl and pour mediterranean olive oil sparingly.
  4. Season the potato pieces with himalayan salt and any other seasoning salt you prefer.
  5. Place the potato pieces onto a baking sheet then into the oven for 25 minutes.
  6. Fill an appropriately sized pot to about halfway with water and set to a boil.
  7. Drop the pasta into the pot once the water is boiling and keep the lid open.
  8. Add a pinch of salt to the mixture and stir.
  9. Cook for 12 minutes and store drain the water once done.
  10. Pour mediterranean olive oil in a pan until it completely covers the surface and set to medium heat.
  11. Drop your french stir fry into the pan and reduce heat as needed.
  12. Cook for 6 minutes and flip regularly.
  13. Dish the pasta, french stir fry, potato pieces and gravy onto a plate and enjoy ;)
